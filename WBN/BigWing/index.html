About Founder

I am Naveen Manickam, Founder of WBN India and a Business Coach. My goal is to help you achieve your personal and business goals with ease and speed. With over 12 years of experience, I have consulted for more than 12 companies and addressed more than 5,000 people in 10+ talks and seminars throughout India. As a keynote speaker and seminar leader, I have addressed more than 2,500 people each year on topics such as Leadership, Sales, Self-Esteem, Goals, Strategy, Creativity, and Success Psychology. My seminars and talks are designed to bring about immediate changes and long-term results. As a business coach, I can help you build your skills and confidence, achieve your goals, and succeed in your industry.
